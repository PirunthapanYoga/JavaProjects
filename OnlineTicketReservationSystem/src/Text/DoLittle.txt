DoLittle

Dolittle is an upcoming American fantasy adventure film directed by Stephen Gaghan, from a screenplay by Gaghan and a story by Thomas Shepherd. The film is based on the character Doctor Dolittle created by Hugh Lofting, and primarily based on The Voyages of Doctor Dolittle. Robert Downey Jr. stars as the title character, alongside Antonio Banderas and Michael Sheen in live action roles; the voice cast includes Emma Thompson, Rami Malek, John Cena, Kumail Nanjiani, Octavia Spencer, Tom Holland, Craig Robinson, Ralph Fiennes, Selena Gomez and Marion Cotillard.

Dolittle is scheduled to be released in the United States on January 17, 2020.
